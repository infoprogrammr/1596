Monsoon Challenge

It is raining almost every day. But Someday It becomes Sunny. Joya (virtual Member ) had planted a tree in her virtual society. The tree grows 10 inch more on a day if it has been raining for the last two days and it will rain the next two days or it has rained for the last three days. * denotes rain and / denotes no rain. You will have to input a string of 10 days only. You have to output what will be the length of the tree. (in inch) In one day it can grow almost 10 inches.

Test Cases:

Input : ***/**//**

output : 10 inch

Explanation: On day 4 , it has been raining for the last three days. 

Input : ******/****

Output : 60 inch
