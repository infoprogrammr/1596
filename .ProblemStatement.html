Monsoon Challenge

Its raining Almost Everyday . But Someday It become Sunny . Joya (virtual Member ) had planted a tree in her virtual society. The tree grows 1 inch more on a day if it has been raining from last two days and it will rain the next two days or it has rained from last three days . * denotes rain and / denotes no rain . You will have to input string of 10 days only . You have to output what will be the lenght of the tree . (in inch) On one day it can grow atmost 10 inch .

output : 10 inch

Explanation  : Since on  day 4 , it has been raining since last three days .
