MARKS

Students of iet lucknow have completed their CTs recently . Everyone is interested in finding only two person's marks . One with the highest one and one with the lowest one . Help students to find out the largest and least marks in the marks array . Their are suppose only 10 students in the class . 

Input 10 integers that represents the marks of students .

You have output lowest and highest marks . Along with that print (students with lowest marks) , (students with highest marks ) in the same line . 

Test Cases:

Input : 23 24 21 26 21 22 24 29 28 29

output : 21 29  (3 5) (8 10)

Explanation  : highest marks : 29 and lowest marks : 21 . Also student 3 , 5 are the one with lowest marks . And 8,10 are with the highest ones .
