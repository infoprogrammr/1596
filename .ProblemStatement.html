Monsoon Challenge

From several days we are enjoying monsoon . Now You are given a task . You will be given an array that represents the amount of rain (in mm) in your area . The length of days will be 10 , since rain is going on from last 10 days . You have to find on how many days the amount of rain has repeated itself .  

Test Cases:

Input : 23 24 21 26 21 22 24 29 28 29

output : 3

Explanation  : Since on day 5 we have same amount as on day 3 . On day 7 same as day 2 . On day 10 same as day 8  .

Thus there are 3 days where the amount of rainfall has been repeated . So the output is 3 .
